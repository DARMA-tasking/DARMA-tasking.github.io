\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Predict an object\textquotesingle{}s load as a sum over blocks of N future phases. 

Expected to be most useful either when queried by an explicitly subphase-\/aware vector-\/optimizing load balancer, or when queried by a whole-\/phase scalar-\/optimizing load balancer with a \mbox{\hyperlink{classvt_1_1vrt_1_1collection_1_1balance_1_1_norm}{Norm}} model composed on top of this.

Multiple phase blocked predictions will only be meaningfully different from single phase predictions when composed on top of a Predictor model that is not constant across future phases. I.\+e. {\ttfamily \mbox{\hyperlink{structvt_1_1vrt_1_1collection_1_1balance_1_1_linear_model}{Linear\+Model}}} rather than {\ttfamily \mbox{\hyperlink{structvt_1_1vrt_1_1collection_1_1balance_1_1_naive_persistence}{Naive\+Persistence}}} or {\ttfamily \mbox{\hyperlink{structvt_1_1vrt_1_1collection_1_1balance_1_1_persistence_median_last_n}{Persistence\+Median\+LastN}}}.
